About CPS Upcycling

Project Overview

CPS Upcycling is a web-based platform developed to promote sustainable thinking through creative problem solving. The system applies the Creative Problem Solving (CPS) approach to help users generate innovative upcycling ideas using common waste materials.

Problem Statement

Many recyclable materials are discarded without exploring their creative potential. A lack of structured guidance and creative thinking tools often prevents individuals from transforming waste into useful and sustainable products.

Creative Problem Solving (CPS)

Creative Problem Solving (CPS) is a structured approach used to identify problems, generate creative ideas, and develop effective solutions related to recycling and upcycling waste materials.

SCAMPER Technique

SCAMPER is a creative thinking technique used to stimulate idea generation through seven structured prompts:

  • S – Substitute
  • C – Combine
  • A – Adapt
  • M – Modify
  • P – Put to another use
  • E – Eliminate
  • R – Reverse

Project Objectives

  • Promote recycling and upcycling awareness
  • Apply the Creative Problem Solving (CPS) approach
  • Encourage creative and sustainable innovation
  • Provide structured guidance for idea generation

Target Users

  • Students and educators
  • Creative individuals
  • Community members
  • Environmental awareness groups
